Modernizing a historical Asian hub

The decrease of foot traffic and anti-Asian sentiment caused by COVID-19 challenges the diverse culture Bay Area claims to be. Asian communities are at risk of survival due to shelter-in-place orders, with closures of family-run eateries, corner stores and other businesses struggling unable to maintain the local economy. The goal is to bring light and offer relief to these Asian-owned businesses, impacted by COVID-19.
