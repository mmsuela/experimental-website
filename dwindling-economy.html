A sense of belonging lost

These historical Asian enclaves serve many purposes to the individuals of its community. These territories act as gateways to new immigrants, guardians of a history on survival and sanctuaries of cultural heritage from discrimination. While businesses are a small sliver of the entire community, it plays a strong role in its local economy in perserving its culture.

90 percent drop in business due to xenophobia

Bay Area Asian-owned stores were hit as early as December 2019 by the impact of COVID-19 because of rising xenophobia and anti-Asian discrimination. More than 70 percent of Asian-owned restaurants reported their inability to survive to the end of 2020 this year, according to the nonprofit Chinatown Community Development Center.
